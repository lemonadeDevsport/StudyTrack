What is Artificial Intelligence?

    Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. AI allows computers and software to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

    This is AI designed to perform a specific task like facial recognition or playing chess.

  • General AI:

    This is an advanced AI that can perform any intellectual task that a human can do.
